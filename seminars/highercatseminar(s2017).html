Higher Category Theory Seminar - Spring 2017

I am organizing a seminar on higher categories. The goal is to familiarize ourselves with a wide range of topics common in the realm of higher categories, as well as witness interesting applications in other areas such as geometry.

We meet at the following times and places:

  • Time: Monday and Wednesday 2 PM
  • Place: 443 Altgeld Hall
